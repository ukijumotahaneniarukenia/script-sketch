- CMD

```
$printf "%s\n" {apple,banana,orange} {100,200,300} {A..C} | xargs -n3 | Rscript -e 's<-readLines("stdin");ss<-strsplit(s," ");names(ss)<-c(1:length(ss));df<-unstack(stack(ss));colnames(df)<-c("item","price","rank");write.csv(df,row.names = FALSE,stdout())'
```
