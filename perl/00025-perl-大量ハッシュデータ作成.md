```
$perl -MParallel::ForkManager -E 'my $pm=Parallel::ForkManager->new(12);%b=();foreach my $e (a..z){$pm->start and next;%b=(%b,($e,1));$pm->finish;};@a=keys %b;say $#a+1'
```

- OUT

```
0
```
